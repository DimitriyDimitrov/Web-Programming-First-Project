How I started.  

I fall inlove in travelling and hiking since 1999 year.I was 6 year old little Mommy's boy.My first trip was to Greece,direction Thessaloniki.I really liked this amazing City full with tourist and old architechture since Greek Empire.I visited also small cities down the road.When I quit Bulgaria for my second trip I was 10 years old.I visited Turkey,one of the biggest city in the world ,,Istanbul'',20 milion people living there,such a great city,beautiful and inspiring.Then I had break for more than 3 years.Then we went for a holiday in Slovakia,Czech,Hungary and Austria.Amazing countries in Central Europe.We was with a car and that helped me to see a lot of cities and Landmarks over the road.After this trip I went alone for a holiday again in those four countries. When I years 14 old me and my familly started living in Spain,Valencia.I stayed there for almost 2 years,and because of that I saw a lot of border Countries next to Spain.For example Andorra,France,Italy,Monaco,Monte-Carlo.And of course a lot of cities like Barcelona,Valencia,Lyon,Genoa,Torino,St.Troppez and more.When I came back home I was so deppressed and because of that I started to go more often for a vacation outside of the country.I choosed Romania,Serbia,Greece,Macedonia and even Slovenija and Croatia.Then 5 years later I went for university in Germany.I stayed there for 3 years and travelled a lot,I visited Switzerland,Lichtenstein,Luxemburg,Netherland,Belgium and more. That's my little story - How I started.

Do you want to join me ?  

If you want to Join me,You should be very prepeared but that's not scary at all!It all start since 4 years ago,when I was 21 years old.In that moment I used to live in Germany,Konstanz the warmest town in Deutschland. When I came back for a summer vacation in my home country Bulgaria I decided to go more often where the Black Sea is and mountains next to the sea.On the road to the sea when I accross mountains like Sakar and Strandzha,some people said they are mysterious mountains but they are wonderfull.If course I fall inlove more with the mountains than the sea! Because of that I started searching for group of people dedicated for hiking and trekkin,and of course I found sooner than expected.With this group we obeyed amazing and awesome mountains like:Rila,Pirin,Vitosha,Rhodopi,Stara Planina,Sakar and Strandzha. Some of them are really hard for trekking and hiking ,it depends of wich season but at the end when you reach the top and enjoy the view you are satisfied.Together we reach some Peaks like:Musala wich is highest on the balkans with 2925m., above sea level,but its not that hard,Vihren 2914m., above sea level wich is more harder than Musala,Koncheto,Todorka,Malyiovitza,Cherni vryh,Kupena and more. Then we decided to explore more of Bulgaria not only Mountains and Peaks.I bought one tourism book for that reason.After that we explore caves like Prohodna,Snejanka,Diavolskoto Gurlo,Ledenika and more,Watterfalls like Slivodol,Fotinovo and more.Many monasteryes and more national Landmarks. So,If you want to Join Us,You are Welcome!We need more people who wants to share some expirience,breathtaking moments and views like thoose I had a pleasure to enjoy!
